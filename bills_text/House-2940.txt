                                 A BILL


 
     To extend the program of block grants to States for temporary 
 assistance for needy families and related programs through September 
                               30, 2019.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. EXTENSION OF THE TEMPORARY ASSISTANCE FOR NEEDY FAMILIES 
              PROGRAM AND RELATED PROGRAMS THROUGH SEPTEMBER 30, 2019.

    Activities authorized by part A of title IV and section 1108(b) of 
the Social Security Act shall continue through September 30, 2019, in 
the manner authorized for fiscal year 2018, and out of any money in the 
Treasury of the United States not otherwise appropriated, there are 
hereby appropriated such sums as may be necessary for such purpose.
