                          AT THE FIRST SESSION

          Begun and held at the City of Washington on Thursday,
           the third day of January, two thousand and nineteen


                                 An Act


 
  To change the address of the postal facility designated in honor of 
                          Captain Humayun Khan.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,
SECTION 1. CAPTAIN HUMAYUN KHAN POST OFFICE.
    Section 1(a) of Public Law 115-347 (132 Stat. 5054) is amended by 
striking ``180 McCormick Road'' and inserting ``2150 Wise Street''.
