                                 AN ACT


 
 To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located 
 at 108 West Maple Street in Holly, Michigan, as the ``Holly Veterans 
                        Memorial Post Office''.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. HOLLY VETERANS MEMORIAL POST OFFICE.

    (a) Designation.--The facility of the United States Postal Service 
located at 108 West Maple Street in Holly, Michigan, shall be known and 
designated as the ``Holly Veterans Memorial Post Office''.
    (b) References.--Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, 
paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to 
in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the ``Holly 
Veterans Memorial Post Office''.

            Passed the House of Representatives September 30, 2020.
