                                 An Act


 
To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 
   509 Fairhope Avenue in Fairhope, Alabama, as the ``William `Jack' 
               Jackson Edwards III Post Office Building''.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,
SECTION 1. WILLIAM ``JACK'' JACKSON EDWARDS III POST OFFICE BUILDING.
    (a) Designation.--The facility of the United States Postal Service 
located at 509 Fairhope Avenue in Fairhope, Alabama, shall be known and 
designated as the ``William `Jack' Jackson Edwards III Post Office 
Building''.
    (b) References.--Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, 
paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to 
in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the ``William 
`Jack' Jackson Edwards III Post Office Building''.
