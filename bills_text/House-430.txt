                                 An Act


 
To extend the program of block grants to States for temporary assistance 
     for needy families and related programs through June 30, 2019.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,
SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.
    This Act may be cited as the ``TANF Extension Act of 2019''.
SEC. 2. EXTENSION OF THE TEMPORARY ASSISTANCE FOR NEEDY FAMILIES 
PROGRAM AND RELATED PROGRAMS THROUGH JUNE 30, 2019.
    Activities authorized by part A of title IV and section 1108(b) of 
the Social Security Act shall continue through June 30, 2019, in the 
manner authorized for fiscal year 2018, and out of any money in the 
Treasury of the United States not otherwise appropriated, there are 
hereby appropriated such sums as may be necessary for such purpose.
