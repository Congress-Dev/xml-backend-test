                                 AN ACT


 
 To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located 
  at 2201 E. Maple Street in North Canton, Ohio, as the ``Lance Cpl. 
                  Stacy `Annie' Dryden Post Office''.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. LANCE CPL. STACY ``ANNIE'' DRYDEN POST OFFICE.

    (a) Designation.--The facility of the United States Postal Service 
located at 2201 E. Maple Street in North Canton, Ohio, shall be known 
and designated as the ``Lance Cpl. Stacy `Annie' Dryden Post Office''.
    (b) References.--Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, 
paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to 
in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the ``Lance Cpl. 
Stacy `Annie' Dryden Post Office''.

            Passed the House of Representatives September 30, 2020.
